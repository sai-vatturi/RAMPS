Create and manage personalized meal plans tailored to individual or family dietary goals, preferences, and restrictions. Schedule meals for the week, ensuring variety and nutritional balance in every plan.

Access detailed nutritional information for each recipe and meal. Track daily nutritional intake, compare it to personal goals, and gain insights into macronutrients and micronutrients for better health management.
